# COVID-19 Impact on U.S. Markets and Industries (2019-2023)

## Project Goal

How did COVID-19 affect certain markets and sectors within the U.S. economy?

This project uses public financial and subscriber data to analyze the effects of COVID-19 on different industries.

## Industry Studied

### Streaming Services

Companies:
- Netflix
- Disney+
- Hulu

Metrics:
- Quarterly Subscribers
- Quarterly Revenue
- Pre-COVID vs COVID vs Post-COVID comparison

## Key Findings

- Streaming services experienced rapid subscriber growth during COVID-19.
- Revenue continued increasing even after subscriber growth slowed.
- Consumer behavior shifted permanently toward digital entertainment.
